Context

The real problem
this solves

Most startup websites look nice but fail to do the one thing they need to do — get a visitor to act.

⚠ The Problem

Startup sites that look good but convert poorly

  • Visitors don't understand the offer in 5 seconds
  • No clear call to action above the fold
  • Looks broken or slow on mobile
  • No social proof to build immediate trust
  • Generic copy that doesn't speak to anyone
✓ The Solution

A conversion-first layout that guides the user

  • Crystal-clear hero with a single strong CTA
  • Visual hierarchy that leads the eye top to bottom
  • Mobile-first and fast — no layout shifts
  • Trust signals: stats, testimonials, social proof
  • Clean code that scales as the product grows
